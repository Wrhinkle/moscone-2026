## The through-line (from the post-event synthesis)

The notes converge on **loop-based AI systems**: agents that observe → condense → decide → act
through tool calls → evaluate → reflect or escalate to a human → improve the next loop. Applied
threads:

- knowledge loops (Memex, Knowledge Navigator, the Firecrawl signal tracker)
- development loops (agent evals, software factories, brownfield agents)
- approval loops (HumanLayer, metareflection/guardians)
- action loops (tool-call-first computing, the tool-call game)
- conversational loops (Pipecat/Daily, Gemini S2S, Tavus)
- learning loops (Learning Loop for Brigid, no-priors education)
