# Acrew Capital

> An early-stage (seed/Series A) venture firm investing across fintech, security, data infrastructure, and applied AI — relevant here as a repeat backer of agentic AI startups (including fellow AIEWF sponsor Veris AI).

- **Category:** vc-community
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2019, San Francisco (Verified — TechCrunch)
- **Website:** https://www.acrewcapital.com

## What they do
Acrew writes seed/Series A checks with a "team-first," thesis-driven approach; ~$1.7B AUM across funds, 146 portfolio companies as of ~2026, including Coinbase, Plaid, Chime, Gusto, Vanta, Protect AI, and Writer (Verified — CB Insights, Acrew site). Not a product to integrate — relevant to engineers as a funding signal and community node.

**Founders:** Theresia Gouw, Lauren Kolodny, Vishal Lugani, Asad Khaliq, Mark Kraynak, co-founded 2019 (Verified — TechCrunch, Wikipedia).
**Funding/scale:** Raised a $700M fund as of the firm's 5-year mark (Oct 2024) (Verified — TechCrunch).

## Evidence of real-world use
Track record: 15 unicorns, 4 IPOs, 32 acquisitions across portfolio (Reported — CB Insights/Tracxn aggregation). Added Aliisa Rosenthal (ex-OpenAI Head of Sales) as GP, signaling deeper AI commercialization focus (Reported).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
As an investor in applied-AI infrastructure (e.g., Protect AI for ML security, Veris AI for agent training), Acrew is a useful lens on which agent-infrastructure bets institutional capital is making — a community/funding signal rather than a technical dependency.
