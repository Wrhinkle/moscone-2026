# Untapped Capital

> A Los Angeles-based, data-driven search fund (pre-seed/seed) that sources ~90% of its deal flow via systematic outbound rather than warm intros, backing under-networked technical founders.

- **Category:** vc-community
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2020, Los Angeles (Reported — VC Sheet, Tracxn)
- **Website:** https://untapped.vc/

## What they do
Untapped Capital targets pre-seed/seed founders in large markets who lack conventional VC network access — "introverted technologists, non-tech industry veterans, and heads-down operators" — sourcing most of its pipeline through systematic outbound search rather than referrals. Reported fund size ~$15M (VC Sheet).

## Founders & funding
Co-founded by Yohei Nakajima (built Techstars' outbound startup-recruiting engine; also known for BabyAGI, an early autonomous-agent open-source project) and Jessica Jackley (co-founder of Kiva.org) (Reported — VC Sheet, Medium/Recast Capital). Note a distinct, similarly-named "Untapped Ventures" (LA-based AI-native VC) also exists in search results; this profile is for **Untapped Capital** specifically per the founders and fund-size data above.

## Evidence of real-world use / relevance
As a search-fund-style investor rather than a product company, "usage" evidence is investor/LP-facing (MFV Partners, Recast Capital coverage) rather than customer case studies. Relevant to this repo's vc-community track as an example of an outbound-sourcing-driven, technically-founder-friendly seed fund; Nakajima's own BabyAGI work gives the fund direct agentic-AI credibility.
