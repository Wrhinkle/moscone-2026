# Towards AI

> An AI-education publication/community — courses, newsletter, and a Discord — not a product company, sponsoring as a community/media node.

- **Category:** vc-community
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2019 (Reported)
- **Website / GitHub:** https://towardsai.net/ · https://newsletter.towardsai.net/ — no GitHub (media/education, not software)

## What they do
Towards AI is an AI-focused publishing and education platform: a newsletter ("This AI newsletter is all you need," read by 130,000+ practitioners), ~40 articles/tutorials a week, paid technical/non-technical AI courses and corporate training, a published book ("Building LLMs for Production"), and the "Learn AI Together" Discord community. In engineer terms it's a media/education business, not something you integrate into a stack — its relevance to a builder is as a learning resource and community channel rather than a tool or vendor.

## Founders & origins
Co-founded by Louis-François Bouchard (also CTO/Head of Community; creator of the "What's AI" YouTube channel since 2020; prior PhD work at Mila/Polytechnique Montreal), along with Roberto Iriondo, Denis Piffaretti, and Louie Peters. (Reported)

## Funding
No external funding rounds found in public sources — appears to run on course/training/newsletter revenue. (Not found in public sources)

## Evidence of real-world use
130,000+ newsletter subscribers and an active Discord community are the primary usage signal; no case studies or enterprise deployments apply since this isn't a software product. (Reported — self-stated audience numbers, not independently verified)

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
Indirect: a widely-read educational channel covering LLM/agent engineering topics, useful as a training resource for teams onboarding engineers to agentic AI rather than as infrastructure.
