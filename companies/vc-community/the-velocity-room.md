## What they do

The Velocity Room runs a tiered (Discover / Implement / Innovate), free-to-join membership program combining a practitioner-written content library (guides, case studies, "intelligence briefings" on AI research/releases), a Slack peer network, live virtual/in-person events (e.g., a June 2026 GPU-CPU co-design session), and early beta access to partner tools. It is positioned as neutral practitioner infrastructure/security community rather than a product or investment vehicle.
