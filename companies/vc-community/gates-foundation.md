# Gates Foundation

> The world's largest private philanthropy, now a funder/partner deploying AI (including via a major Anthropic grant) toward global health and development outcomes rather than a startup or product vendor.

- **Category:** vc-community
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2000, Seattle, WA (Verified — as the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation)
- **Website:** https://www.gatesfoundation.org

## What they do
Not a company an engineer integrates with — the Gates Foundation is a grantmaking philanthropy that funds AI applied to global health, agriculture, and education, and increasingly funds/partners with AI labs directly. In May 2026 it announced a $200M, four-year partnership with Anthropic providing grant funding, API credits, and technical support to build shared AI tools across health, education, and agriculture. It also runs an "AI for Equity" program and an AI Fellows initiative (12-month engineering/ML fellowships building dashboards, diagnostic models, and agents for LMIC health data), plus grants (up to $100K each) to ~48 projects applying AI to global health problems, mostly in Africa. One-line founders/funding note: founded by Bill and Melinda Gates (with Warren Buffett as a major later donor); it's an endowed foundation, not a funding round-raising entity (Verified).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
Sits in the "vc-community"/funder category as a non-traditional capital source — grant funding and compute/API credits rather than equity — for engineers building agentic tools aimed at public-good and global-health back-office problems (diagnostics, data pipelines, agent-assisted case triage).
