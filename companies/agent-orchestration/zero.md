# Zero

## Disambiguation note

"Zero" collides with several distinct products: **Zero Email/Orchid** (YC S25, Adam Wazzan & Nizar Abi Zaher, AI email client — its old YC page now shows "Orchid"), and **ZeroClick** (Ryan Hudson's $55M AI ad-network startup, ex-Honey team). The AIEWF 2026 sponsor page links directly to **zero.xyz**, which this profile covers; Product Hunt listed **Gabe Perez, Daniel Baum, and Ryan Hudson** as its launch team (Reported), suggesting overlap with the ZeroClick/Honey team but a functionally separate product.

## What they do

Zero (zero.xyz) works with Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, and other CLI agents; the agent "discovers" and calls whichever indexed service it needs (images, email, scraping, deploys) rather than requiring pre-configured API keys. Uses an HTTP 402-style pay-per-call model; no platform markup, per the company.

## Evidence of real-world use

#3 Product of the Day on Product Hunt (~360 upvotes) via organic word of mouth; 8,600+ to 26,000+ indexed services reported across sources (Reported, company/PH self-reported, figures inconsistent).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering

Tool-calling/discovery layer for agent stacks — addresses the "agent needs a tool it wasn't configured for" gap.
