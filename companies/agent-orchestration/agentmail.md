# AgentMail

> Email inbox API built for AI agents — spin up a real, threaded email address for an agent in milliseconds, no domain setup required.

- **Category:** agent-orchestration
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2025, San Francisco (YC S25 batch) (**Verified**)
- **Website / GitHub:** https://www.agentmail.to · https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/agentmail

## What they do
AgentMail is an API-first, fully-managed email platform for agentic workflows: create an inbox via REST call, and the agent can send, receive, thread, label, search, and reply to real email — SPF/DKIM/DMARC handled automatically, with realtime webhooks and semantic search over messages. The pitch is that agents need email as an identity/auth primitive (most SaaS signup and account-recovery flows gate on email), and traditional transactional-email APIs (SendGrid, etc.) aren't built for two-way, agent-driven conversation threads.

**Founders (Verified):** Haakam Aujla (ex-Optiver quant), Michael Kim (ex-Nvidia autonomous vehicles), Adi Singh (ex-Accel/StepStone/Flex Capital) — University of Michigan grads.

**Funding (Verified):** $6M seed, March 2026, led by General Catalyst; participation from Y Combinator, Phosphor Capital, and angels including Paul Graham, Dharmesh Shah (HubSpot), Paul Copplestone (Supabase), Karim Atiyeh (Ramp).

## Evidence of real-world use
Reports over 100M+ emails delivered, tens of thousands of human users, hundreds of thousands of agent users, and 500+ B2B customers; usage reportedly spiked 3-4x following the "OpenClaw" agent launch in early 2026.

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
Fills a specific gap in the agent tool-calling stack: giving autonomous agents a persistent identity/communication channel for browser automation, customer service, and outreach agents — adjacent to voice/SMS agent infra and agent-identity work in this repo.
