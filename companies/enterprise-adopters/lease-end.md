# Lease End

> A direct-to-consumer fintech that automates the entire car-lease buyout process online, and has rebuilt its funnel around in-house agentic AI.

- **Category:** enterprise-adopters
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** company based in Burley/Lehi, Utah region (Unverified exact year); scaled to 101–250 employees (Reported — Crunchbase)
- **Website:** https://www.leaseend.com

## What they do
Lease End lets drivers end an auto lease — buy it out, refinance it, or trade it in — entirely online instead of through a dealership: soft credit check, VIN/plate lookup, financing offers from a partner lender network, and paperwork/title transfer handled digitally. In 2025 they layered on an "AI Constellation" of in-house agentic systems covering the funnel end to end: an AI Lease Buyout Calculator (chat interface producing a payment estimate from name/address + VIN in ~1 minute) and **Arco**, a voice-powered AI sales agent that walks drivers through financing terms and can complete a buyout without a human rep unless requested (Verified — Businesswire press releases).

## Founders & funding
Co-founded by Brandon Williams (CEO, previously co-founder/chairman of Doba) and Zander Cook (COO, BYU economics). Public funding-round details were not found in available sources (Unverified).

## Evidence of real-world use
Lease End states it delivered $75.7M in consumer savings in 2024 and built the AI Constellation tools "entirely" with its in-house tech team, not third-party vendors — a real production deployment, not a pilot (Reported — company press releases).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
A concrete enterprise case of a voice + chat agent taking an existing manual, phone/dealership-driven sales workflow and turning it into an autonomous closer — relevant to voice/realtime agent design and to agentic-commerce patterns discussed at AIEWF.
