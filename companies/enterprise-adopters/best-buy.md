# Best Buy

> A consumer electronics retailer that has deployed generative and agentic AI in production across customer support, contact-center tooling, and in-store service — a large-scale enterprise adopter, not a tool vendor.

- **Category:** enterprise-adopters
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 1966, Richfield, MN (Verified — public company)
- **Website / GitHub:** https://www.bestbuy.com / https://corporate.bestbuy.com (no engineering GitHub; not a dev-tools company)

## What they do
Best Buy runs a gen-AI virtual assistant (built on Google Cloud Vertex AI / Gemini models, with Accenture as systems integrator) embedded in BestBuy.com, its app, and phone support, handling troubleshooting, order/delivery changes, and subscription management as self-service. Internally, agent-assist tooling does real-time conversation summarization and sentiment detection for contact-center staff — reportedly deployed in six to eight weeks. As of 2025 the company said it's expanding to agentic AI in-store: Geek Squad technicians and floor associates using AI for diagnostics, product recommendations, and predictive service. For engineers this is a case study in retail-scale RAG/assist deployment on a major cloud LLM stack rather than a novel technology.
