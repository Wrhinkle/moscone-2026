## What they do
Bloomberg's ML Product & Research group, together with its AI Engineering team, built **BloombergGPT**, a 50-billion-parameter LLM trained from scratch on a 363B-token mixed corpus (financial documents + public data) for finance-specific NLP: sentiment analysis, NER, news classification, financial Q&A, and summarization. Bloomberg reports it outperforms general LLMs on financial-summary benchmarks. Beyond the model itself, Bloomberg's AI Engineering group is actively publishing applied agent research — e.g., an improved agent tool-calling methodology presented at ACL 2025, and eight papers at ACL 2026 spanning agentic systems, responsible AI, and financial NLP. Engineer takeaway: a case study in building a domain-specific foundation model in-house plus operationalizing agentic tool-calling in a regulated, high-stakes (financial) production environment.
