# SonderMind

> A behavioral-health platform matching patients to therapists/psychiatrists, now layering AI tools on top of the human care network (clinical documentation, care navigation).

- **Category:** enterprise-adopters
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2014, Denver, CO (Verified — Tracxn/Fierce Healthcare)
- **Website:** https://www.sondermind.com/ai/

## What they do
SonderMind is a consumer-facing mental health marketplace/provider network (virtual + in-person therapy and psychiatry, insurance-integrated, now available in all 50 states) that has added an AI layer rather than being AI-native. Its "Care Concierge" uses AI to match and schedule patients with therapists/psychiatrists across insurance networks. Its "AI Notes" tool is the more technically notable piece: an ambient clinical-documentation assistant that cuts session-note completion time from ~20 minutes to ~4 (an 80% reduction, per company claim), reportedly saving providers up to 90 minutes/day — a concrete case of LLM summarization applied to a regulated, high-stakes documentation workflow.

**Founders & funding:** Founded by Mark Frank (CEO) and Sean Boyd (Verified). Raised $276M total including a $150M Series C (2021) led by Drive Capital/Premji Invest that pushed it to unicorn ($1B+) valuation; earlier rounds from General Catalyst, Kickstart Seed Fund, F-Prime Capital (Verified — Fierce Healthcare, company announcements).

**Evidence of real-world use:** 12,000 contracted providers per company figures; company reports 77% of patients using its digital tools sustain therapy gains 2+ years — self-reported, not independently audited, so treat clinical-outcome numbers as Reported rather than Verified.

**Relevance to agentic AI engineering:** A useful example of AI applied to a regulated, compliance-heavy documentation workflow (ambient scribing/summarization) rather than full agentic autonomy — relevant to healthcare-vertical LLM deployment patterns and human-in-the-loop documentation agents.
