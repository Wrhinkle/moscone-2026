# Accenture

> A global systems-integrator/consultancy that builds and deploys agentic AI for enterprise clients at scale, now running a dedicated joint business unit with Anthropic to embed Claude across regulated industries.

- **Category:** enterprise-adopters
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 1989 (spun off from Andersen Consulting), Dublin/global (Verified — public record)
- **Website:** https://www.accenture.com · https://newsroom.accenture.com

## What they do
Accenture's **AI Refinery** is an agent-building platform (Agent Builder, Trusted Agent Huddle, SDKs) built on NVIDIA AI Enterprise, used to assemble and orchestrate industry-specific agent teams — 50+ shipped, targeting 100 by year-end. It's less a product engineers integrate directly and more a delivery vehicle: Accenture forward-deployed engineers embed inside client environments to build and operationalize agents (Verified — Accenture newsroom).

## Founders & funding
N/A — public company (NYSE: ACN), not a startup.

## Evidence of real-world use
Named clients/partners: ESPN, HPE, the UN (via AI Refinery); 450+ Accenture-built agents listed on Google Cloud Marketplace (Verified — Accenture newsroom). The **Accenture-Anthropic Business Group** commits to training ~30,000 professionals on Claude/Claude Code — one of the largest Claude-practitioner ecosystems reported anywhere (Verified — Anthropic + Accenture joint press releases).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering
As an integrator, Accenture is a bellwether for enterprise agent adoption patterns (governance, forward-deployed engineering, regulated-industry rollout) relevant to this repo's back-office-automation and enterprise-adopters research areas.
