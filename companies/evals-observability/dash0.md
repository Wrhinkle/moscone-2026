## What they do
Dash0 is a logs/metrics/traces observability platform built on OpenTelemetry (plus Prometheus and Perses for dashboards), sold on "observability as code" (version-controlled dashboards/alerts) and usage-based pricing tied to actual telemetry volume, not seats or byte size. Its AI-native piece, **Agent0**, is pitched as an "autonomous engineer for production": it scans services, correlates telemetry to investigate incidents end-to-end, and ships validated fixes as draft pull requests.

## Founders, funding & evidence of use
