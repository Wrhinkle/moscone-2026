## What they do
Reactor builds a developer platform for real-time "world models": a unified SDK/API that generates interactive video/environments live, in response to user input, rather than the multi-minute batch generation typical of text-to-video systems (Reactor claims near-zero time-to-first-frame). Positioned for media/entertainment, physical AI, and robotics use cases; AWS is a strategic infrastructure/distribution partner.

## Founders & funding
Co-founded by Alberto Taiuti (CEO, ex-Apple Vision Pro technical lead, previously co-founded Luma AI) and Bryce Schmidtchen (CTO, also ex-Apple Vision Pro) — Verified via multiple press outlets. Raised $59M Series A led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, with WndrCo (Jeffrey Katzenberg), Amplify Partners, Sky9 Capital, Abstract Ventures, and FPV Ventures participating, announced May 2026 alongside emergence from stealth.
