## What they do
uRun (urun.sh) markets itself around the tagline "the runtime is coming" and positions as infrastructure for real-time, continuously-running AI video: developers build experiences — interactive scenes, "AI companions," multi-character worlds, adaptive narratives — that generate and render live rather than as pre-rendered clips, steerable in-the-moment via voice, text, or webcam input, with state persisting across scenes/streams. In engineer terms this sits in the same space as real-time generative video/world models with an agent-and-voice-driven control layer, positioning it adjacent to voice/realtime interaction stacks rather than batch video generation.
