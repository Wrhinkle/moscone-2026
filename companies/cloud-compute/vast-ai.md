# Vast.ai

> A GPU compute marketplace/cloud that lets buyers rent GPUs (from hobbyist rigs to data-center racks) at below-hyperscaler prices from a network of independent hosts.

- **Category:** cloud-compute
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** silver
- **Founded:** Incorporated June 2016 (Verified — company "About" page)
- **Website / GitHub:** https://vast.ai · https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/vastai

## What they do

Vast.ai runs a marketplace connecting GPU hosts (gaming rigs, mining farms, small data centers, and larger hosts) with renters needing on-demand compute, undercutting AWS/GCP/Azure pricing. Products include on-demand and interruptible instances, templated container deployment, and "Vast Finance" GPU financing for hosts. You'd use it as a cheaper alternative/supplement to hyperscaler GPU instances for training, fine-tuning, and inference workloads.

## Evidence of real-world use

Company reports 310% growth in 2024, 17,000+ GPUs, 350+ hosts, 40+ employees, SOC 2 Type I certified (Reported — company site, not independently confirmed).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering

Low-cost compute substrate for training/fine-tuning and self-hosted inference behind agent stacks; competes with RunPod, Lambda, CoreWeave.
