# Revenium

> Real-time metering and budget/anomaly control for AI spend — traces every model call, agent turn and workflow to the cent across providers.

- **Category:** fintech-payments
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** silver
- **Website:** https://www.revenium.ai/ (also revenium.io) · formerly **HyperCurrent**

## What they do

Revenium is a no-code-change metering layer that sits alongside LLM/agent calls and aggregates spend, token usage and agent-action counts across providers into a single cost/ROI ledger. It supports hard budget limits, threshold alerts, and auto-shutoff for runaway or looping agents, plus anomaly detection for unusual usage patterns, and ties cost data to business-outcome metrics (TCO/COGS/ROI) for finance stakeholders. Integrations span Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, Databricks, LangChain, Ollama, GitHub, Arize, Datadog, Snowflake and ClickHouse (Verified — company site).

## Founders & funding

Partnership/integration with **SUSE** for agent-driven infrastructure cost monitoring reported in press; no independently-verified named enterprise customer case studies found (Reported).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering

FinOps-for-agents: cost attribution, budget enforcement and loop/anomaly detection are load-bearing for any team running autonomous agents at scale in production.
