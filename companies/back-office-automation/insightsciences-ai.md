## What they do
InsightSciences models "synthetic personas" — AI-generated stand-ins for target audience segments — grounded in a client's prior completed surveys, interviews, or focus groups, so a team can ask new follow-up questions or test new concepts against that context without commissioning fresh primary research. They offer this three ways: bespoke research projects, "Research Replay" (reusing/extending dormant research assets a client already owns), and white-label/behind-the-scenes technology partnerships for other research firms. In engineer terms: a domain-specific RAG/persona-simulation layer over a company's proprietary research corpus, positioned as an insights/ops accelerant rather than a general chatbot.
