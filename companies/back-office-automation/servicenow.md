# ServiceNow

> Enterprise workflow platform (the "Now Platform") for IT service management, HR, and ops — now building agentic AI that acts autonomously across those workflows, reinforced by its 2025 acquisition of Moveworks.

- **Category:** back-office-automation
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2004, Santa Clara/San Diego, CA (Verified — Wikipedia; originally incorporated as Glidesoft in 2003)
- **Website:** https://www.servicenow.com/ai/what-is-agentic-ai.html

## What they do
ServiceNow is the dominant enterprise IT-service-management (ITSM) and workflow platform, extending into HR, customer service, and general "back office" case/request handling. Its AI Agent Studio lets customers build custom agents on the Now Platform via a low-code/natural-language interface, with agents that reason over context, choose actions, and execute across ITSM, ITOM, HR service delivery, and customer service workflows under governance guardrails (traceable, explainable actions per company docs). Its 2025 acquisition of Moveworks (its largest acquisition to date) added a conversational, agentic front-end already deployed across 350+ enterprises and 5.5M+ employees, folding a proven agent product directly into the platform.

**Founders & funding:** Founded by Fred Luddy (Verified); publicly traded since 2012, market cap in the tens of billions — no private funding rounds relevant today.

**Evidence of real-world use:** Deeply embedded in Fortune 500 IT/HR operations; Moveworks' pre-acquisition customer base (350+ enterprises, 5.5M+ employees) is independently documented, not just marketing.

**Relevance to agentic AI engineering:** Directly on-thesis for back-office automation agents — bounded-autonomy agents over structured enterprise workflows, governance/audit trails, and human-in-the-loop escalation are core design patterns this repo tracks (e.g., Job Packet Readiness Auditor-style demos).
