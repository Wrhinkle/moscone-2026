# Veris (Veris AI)

> A simulation-and-reinforcement-learning platform that trains and evaluates enterprise AI agents in high-fidelity synthetic environments before they touch production.

## What they do
Veris AI builds dynamic simulation environments — mixing synthetic scenarios, verifiable reward functions, and production logs — so teams can train and stress-test agents via reinforcement learning and experience rather than prompt engineering alone, then certify behavior before deployment. Available on Google Cloud Marketplace.

**Founders:** CEO Mehdi Jamei, CTO Andi Partovi (Reported — Businesswire).
**Funding:** Emerged from stealth June 2025 with $8.5M seed, backed by Decibel Ventures and **Acrew Capital** (also an AIEWF sponsor — worth noting the overlap) (Reported).
