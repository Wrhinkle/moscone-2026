# TypeSafe AI

> An early-stage SF startup building a new class of LLMs ("System1") meant for reliable, no-human-in-the-loop machine-to-machine execution rather than chat.

- **Category:** models-inference
- **AIEWF 2026 tier:** supporting
- **Founded:** 2024, San Francisco (Reported)
- **Website:** https://typesafe.ai/

## What they do
TypeSafe AI's pitch is "Reliable AI": instead of bolting guardrails onto a general chat model, they say they're rebuilding the model stack from first principles for environments where correctness and latency are non-negotiable — real-time decision systems, compliance pipelines, and autonomous agents operating without a human supervising each step. Their model, System1, targets composable, embeddable "AI decision systems at scale" rather than conversational assistants. As of this research, the site runs a waitlist/careers page — pre-launch or early access, no public docs/pricing found.

## Founders & funding
Founded by Diogo Almeida (CEO, ex-OpenAI, co-invented InstructGPT/RLHF), Erik Gafni (CTO, ex-Freenome/Invitae), and Sasha Sheng (COO, ex-Meta FAIR) — Reported via team page/LinkedIn. Participated in the AWS Generative AI Accelerator (Sept. 2024, Reported); no disclosed funding amount found in public sources.
