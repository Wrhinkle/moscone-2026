## Disambiguation note

"Emissary" collides with **Emissary.ai**, an unrelated recruiting text-messaging SaaS company (founded 2017, NY). This profile covers **withemissary.com / Emissary Technologies Inc.**, the AI-model/eval company, which matches the AIEWF sponsor link and the "models-inference" category.

## What they do

Emissary offers a "FineTune AI Service" for enterprises to build and productionize custom AI models — training, evaluation, and monitoring — pitched as "AI for the 99%," making Silicon Valley-grade model-building practices accessible to non-frontier-lab companies. Team claims "10+ years AI experience" and "200+ models in production" across social, fintech, and search domains (Reported, self-stated).

## Founders & funding

Founder names not disclosed in public sources found (Unverified). Backed by **K5 Global**, **Unusual Ventures**, and **Afore Capital** (Reported — company site); one source cites a $1.2M convertible note from K5 Global (Reported, single-source).

## Evidence of real-world use

Landing page displays Columbia, TikTok, MongoDB, Neeva, TurtleMint, Stanford, HPE logos — not confirmed as active customers vs. past affiliations (treat as weak "logos on a page" evidence).

## Relevance to agentic AI engineering

Custom fine-tuning and ML-grade eval infrastructure for teams building/serving their own models behind agents, rather than relying solely on general-purpose APIs.
